Detailed Description

JointsTrackingTask is a concrete implementation of the Task.

Please use this element if you want to control the desired joint position of the robot. The task represents the following equation

\[ \begin{bmatrix} 0_6 & I_n \end{bmatrix} \nu = \ddot{s} ^ * \]

where \(0_6\) and \(I_n\) are the zero and the identity matrix. The desired joint acceleration is chosen such that the joint will converge to the desired trajectory and it is computed with a standard standard PD controller in \(\mathbb{R}^n\).
